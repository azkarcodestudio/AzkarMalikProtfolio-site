About Me

Web Developer

I have a background in Computer Science and I'm a passionate web developer with a strong focus on Frontend Developer. I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GSAP, and Spline (3D Design), and also have skills in sketching and editing to create visually engaging and interactive user interfaces.
